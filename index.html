PotterVerse

PotterVerse is a visualization to explore how realistic is the Harry Potter universe.

There are 12 times more stable relations (T3,T1) than unstable (T2, T0). Unlike real undirected social networks where the ratio is around 5.

The network is dense with 65 characters and 325 edges. Unlike real networks that are sparse.

The diameter of the network is only 4.
The average path length of PotterVerse is 2.07. In a real social network, the number is closer to 6.
