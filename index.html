PotterVerse

PotterVerse is a visualization to explore the Harry Potter universe.

We are testing if the Potter universe follows the structural balance theory (SBT). SBT says that real social networks have mainly triads such as T3, T1 where the triads represent "friend of a friend is my friend" and the "enemy of my enemy is my friend". On the other hand, triads T2, T4 are less likely to happen in reality.
